About

My name is Kyle Strem and I am a Web Developer. After a year or so of messing around with different programming atmospheres, I finally decided to buckle up and start pursuing a career in web development. I took on months of driven study and was able to get my Front End Development Certificate from FreeCodeCamp.

Now I am out on the prowl and hunting for work. In my off time I am currently pursuing the completion of the FreeCodeCamp course to be certified in Full Stack JavaScript. Alongside that I am an avid basketball fan, a technical enthusiast, and a voracious learner for expanding my programming toolkit.
